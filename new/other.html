Intern, Illumination Entertainment

My 2011 internship at Illumination Entertainment provided inestimable value for me as an aspiring filmmaker. Reading scripts and writing coverage helped me develop one invaluable skill: an eye for story, which lets me see the potential of an idea still in its infancy or the strengths and deficiencies of my own work.

http://illuminationentertainment.com/

Fundraising and Public Relations, Faces of Others International Film Festival 2012

The Faces of Others International Film Festival 2012 aimed to serve as a bridge between Pittsburgh and the international film circuit by presenting the best of world cinema. To bring the festival to life, my partner and I invented an effective marketing plan and raised over $45,000 from a diverse group of corporate and nonprofit sponsors.

http://www.cmu.edu/faces/

Vice President, CMU Filmmaking Club

Comprised mostly of artists and writers, the CMU Film Club continues a fifteen year tradition of unifying dozens of undergraduates who share a common interest in filmmaking. It gave me the opportunity to collaborate with a select group of people whose drive for success is palpable and unrelenting; people with attitudes that beg, "What is your next project, and how can I become involved?"

http://www.cmufilmclub.com/

Fifth Year Scholar

Recently accepted to the Fifth Year Scholarship program, I will be commissioned to stay at Carnegie Mellon University for an additional year in order to create a documentary film series that spotlights student academic and social life. I treat this as an opportunity not only to examine captivating and elusive subject matter but also to refine my filmmaking skills.

http://www.studentaffairs.cmu.edu/dean/fifth/
