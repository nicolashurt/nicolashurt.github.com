About Me

I am currently a Junior in the Tepper School of Business at Carnegie Mellon University, where I am pursuing a customized track in “Film Production Management” and minoring in Film and Media Studies. Before college, I was part of three student film crews which shot on-location in Utah, North Dakota, and Ireland, and I have interned for two summers at Illumination Entertainment in Santa Monica, California. In addition to being a coordinator of fundraising and marketing for the Faces of Others International Film Festival, I am heavily involved in the CMU filmmaking club, for which I have produced multiple short films. My life goal is to be a Hollywood film director whose name is recognized by people in Wyoming.
